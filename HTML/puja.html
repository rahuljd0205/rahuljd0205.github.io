Setting up the Puja

We provide all the basic pre-requisites needed for the puja as follows


  • Panditji
  • The Chaurang
  • The Makar
  • The Panchamrut/Charanamrit

Oil lamp (samai), Ghee Lamp (Niranjan), coconuts, bel patra and all the reamaining ingredients.

Flowerpots/vases of assorted flowers, lights, door hanging (Toran) and any other decoration as per your requirements.

We can arrange for speakers with instrumental music like sitar, Shehnai, Santoor, flute, etc. to create a holy, spriritual atmosphere.

A photographer to save all these memories.
